Day 7 of 7 AIs I use as a PM:

Gamma.

A PM's job is not just to make decisions.

It's to make decisions travel.

Across leadership.

Design.

Engineering.

Growth.

Sales.

Customers.

Investors.

Everyone needs a slightly different version of the same story.

That's where Gamma helps.

I use it when I need to turn strategy into a clean narrative.

Launch plans.

Product updates.

Research summaries.

Roadmap presentations.

Stakeholder decks.

Because sometimes the idea is good, but the packaging is terrible.

And packaging matters.

Not in a fake way.

In a clarity way.

A strong presentation helps people understand:

What problem are we solving?

Why now?

Who is this for?

What did we learn?

What are we doing next?

What decision do we need?

That's not fluff.

That's alignment.

Gamma helps speed up the first version so I can spend more time sharpening the story.

The lesson:

In product, communication is not a soft skill.

It's infrastructure.

If people don't understand the bet, they won't help you win it.
