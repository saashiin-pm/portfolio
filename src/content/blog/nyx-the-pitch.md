Most people go to SF for the weekend and hit Dolores Park.

We went and built an AI that might save you $15,000.

Four builders. Six hours. One hackathon. PMs in AI.

Let's go.

## The backstory

I'm actually thinking about moving to SF.

So like any normal person I started apartment hunting.

Found a place. $3,150/mo. Beautiful photos. Great vibes. I'm in.

Then I looked closer.

- $275 — parking (nearest garage)
- $140 — utilities
- $35 — trash fee (buried in clause 14.3, lol)
- $45 — pest control (mandatory)
- $15 — package locker (can't opt out)

Real rent: **$3,660.**

That's $6,120 a year nobody told me about.

That's a round trip to Tokyo I'll never take.

Cool.

But it gets better.

The gym I picked the whole neighborhood for? 25 min by transit. I'd quit by month two and we both know it.

Management company runs 14 buildings — tenants in 9 of them say repairs take 3 weeks.

Block gets loud Thursday through Saturday.

I'm a light sleeper.

Awesome.

## Here's the wild part

All of this info is **public**.

Building violations. Noise complaints. Tenant reviews on Reddit and Google.

It's just scattered across 10+ sources nobody has time to check.

So you sign the lease.

Cross your fingers.

Find out the hard way.

The hard way costs $15,050.

(Early termination + forfeited deposit + new deposit + movers + overlap rent. Fun.)

## What we built

So this weekend we said screw it, let's build the thing that should already exist.

We built **Nyx**. *Know the city before it knows you.*

Paste a listing. 60 seconds. Nyx pulls violations, block-level noise data, management reviews across their whole portfolio, hidden fees, parking, amenities matched to YOUR life.

No dashboard. No scores. It just talks to you like a friend who's lived in SF for 10 years and won't let you sign a bad lease.

It walks you through your actual Wednesday — gym, commute, coffee, when the block gets loud, what dinner looks like.

A movie of your life there. Not a spreadsheet.

Then one call:

→ **Go** — move fast.

→ **Negotiate** — here's your leverage.

→ **Walk away** — here's why.

People make $40K decisions on listing photos and vibes.

We built the antidote to vibes.

In six hours.

On a Saturday.

If you've ever moved somewhere and thought "why did nobody tell me" — comment below. We'll send you the MVP.
