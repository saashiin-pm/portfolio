7 AIs I use as a PM to move faster.

Not to look productive.

Actually move faster.

There's a difference.

Most people use AI like a better Google search.

Ask a question.

Get an answer.

Move on.

But the real unlock is using AI as a thinking layer across your workflow.

As a PM, my job is basically controlled chaos:

User research.

PRDs.

Analytics.

Design reviews.

Standups.

Launch planning.

Stakeholder updates.

Competitive research.

Random "can we ship this by Friday?" moments.

AI helps me turn that chaos into momentum.

Here are 7 tools I keep coming back to:

1. **ChatGPT** — for thinking, writing, breaking down messy problems, and turning scattered notes into structure.

2. **Notion AI** — for organizing docs, meeting notes, product plans, and internal knowledge.

3. **Figma AI** — for speeding up design exploration and turning early ideas into something visual.

4. **Otter.ai** — for capturing conversations so I can actually listen during user interviews instead of pretending to type like a court reporter.

5. **Mixpanel** — for understanding what users actually do, not what we hope they do.

6. **Lovable** — for turning product ideas into quick prototypes without waiting two weeks to see if the idea even makes sense.

7. **Gamma** — for turning strategy into clean presentations when the idea needs to travel beyond the product team.

The big lesson:

AI does not replace product judgment.

It amplifies it.

If your thinking is messy, AI gives you polished mess.

If your questions are shallow, AI gives you confident shallow answers.

But if you know what problem you're solving, who you're solving it for, and what decision you're trying to make…

AI becomes leverage.

The future PM won't be the one who uses the most tools.

It'll be the one who knows where each tool fits in the workflow.
